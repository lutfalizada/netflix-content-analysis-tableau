# Netflix Content Analysis Dashboard

This project explores Netflix content using Tableau to understand trends in content production.

Key insights analyzed:

• Distribution of Movies vs TV Shows  
• Growth of Netflix content over time  
• Most common genres on the platform  
• Top countries producing Netflix content  

Tableau Dashboard:

## Key Insights
### 1. Movies dominate the Netflix catalog
The dataset shows that Movies significantly outnumber TV Shows on Netflix. This suggests Netflix has historically focused more on movie content, although TV series have grown rapidly in recent years.

### 2. Rapid content expansion after 2016

The number of titles added to Netflix increased dramatically starting around 2016, reaching a peak between 2018 and 2020. This reflects Netflix’s aggressive global expansion and investment in original content.

### 3. Drama and documentary categories are the most common
Genres related to **Dramas**, **International Movies**, and **Documentaries** appear most frequently in the catalog. This indicates strong demand for storytelling-driven content and global productions.

### 4. The United States leads content production
The United States produces the largest share of Netflix titles by a wide margin. Other significant contributors include **India**, **United Kingdom**, and **South Korea**, showing Netflix's growing international production strategy.
